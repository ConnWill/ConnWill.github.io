Early Uncertainty to Present Perfection

The sport of Rugby is a rising but not very well known sport in the United States. I was first introduced to it during middle

school by an old friend of mine. I thought it would be nice to have a spring sport that could get me in shape just in time for

football season. Turns out this sport called rugby became the sport I as best at. Not only did we win multiple state championships

,

but I myself had multiple all-state honors.



This was the sport where I crossed that threshold between working hard and

playing hard. This is the sport where I finally felt a massive amount of enjoyment as a result of working my tail off. Every

time I stepped on that field I knew I was the best player out there, and I knew that I had earned it. This sport gave me a

huge boost of confidence not only in that craft, but in my mind.
