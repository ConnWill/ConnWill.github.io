Will Zachary Conn 

The Beginning

I was born at the Loyola University Hospital in Chicago Illinois, where I received the name Will not William , WILL . I

grew up in LaGrange, Illinois which is about 20 minutes southwest of the City of Chicago. I did not grow up in a very

strict household, and was given plenty of freedom to do what my heart desired. Despite the amount of freedom I was

given to follow my passions, I started picking up all the sports my older brother Peter was playing. The first Christmas

gift I can remember is a Little Tikes basketball hoop, which saw nonstop use from the day I got it until the day it was

destroyed about 5-10 years later. I believe sports not only made me physically tougher but mentally as well. The biggest

net gain of playing sports and being apart of so many different activities was the fact it introduced me to many of my

lifelong friends.
